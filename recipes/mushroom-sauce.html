Ingredients

  • 2 Brown Onions
  • 500g diced button mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon crushed chilli
  • 1 and a half tablespoons of crushed garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 2 packets of Royco Cream of Mushroom sauce
  • 500ml Full Cream milk
  • 250ml cream
  • Salt and pepper to tast

Method

  1. Cook the 2 brown onions (diced) until translucent in a large sauce pan
  2. Add the garlic, chilli and ginger. Cook for an additional 1 min, stirring frequently
  3. Add the mushrooms and cook until all the moisture has evoporated
  4. Mix the 2 packets of Royco Cream of Mushroom sauce with the milk and add to the pan.
  5. Add the cream
  6. Continue stirring whilst the sauce is simmering. For about 15 min.
  7. To increase thickness, add milk and cornflour. To decrease thickness, add water or milk.
  8. Once the desired consitency has been reached, serve with a medium rare fillet.
